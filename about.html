ABOUT
Hi there! Welcome to my site. If you haven’t noticed, my name is Hannah Bullion. I’m currently a sophomore at Michigan State University studying professional writing. I hope to one day make my way to New York City and work in fashion editorial, but until then, I’m working hard here in East Lansing. Currently, I am a communications and social media intern for the College of Arts & Letters here on campus, the social media director for VIM Magazine— our on campus, student run, fashion and lifestyle magazine. I’m also a Style Guru and social media intern for CollegeFashionista. I’m quick learner, hard worker, and all around excited about life gal! If you’d like to contact me, feel free to drop me a line via email at bullionhannah@gmail.com.
